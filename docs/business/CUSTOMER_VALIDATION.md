## Post-Funding Validation Plan

### Phase 1: Beta Program (Months 1-6)

**Objective**: Validate product-market fit with early adopters

| Activity | Target | Timeline |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Beta user recruitment | 50-100 users | Months 1-3 |
| Feedback collection | Weekly surveys | Ongoing |
| Feature iteration | Bi-weekly releases | Ongoing |
| Usage analytics | Dashboard deployment | Month 1 |

**Beta User Criteria**:
- Technical users comfortable with early-stage software
- Genuine use case for AI coordination
- Willingness to provide feedback
- Representative of target segments

### Phase 2: Pilot Programs (Months 4-9)

**Objective**: Generate paying customers and case studies

| Program | Target Customers | Pricing | Duration |
|---------|-----------------|---------|----------|
| University Pilot | 3-5 research institutions | Nominal ($1K-5K) | 6 months |
| Enterprise Pilot | 2-3 enterprises | Standard pricing | 3-6 months |
| Startup Program | 5-10 startups | Freemium | 6 months |

**Pilot Success Metrics**:
- Active usage (weekly)
- Feature adoption
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Conversion intent

### Phase 3: Production Launch (Months 6-12)

**Objective**: Scale to commercial customers

| Metric | Month 6 | Month 9 | Month 12 |
|--------|---------|---------|----------|
| Active Users | 100+ | 500+ | 1,000+ |
| Paying Customers | 1-3 | 3-5 | 5-10 |
| ARR | $50K | $200K | $500K |
| NPS | 30+ | 40+ | 50+ |

## Customer Acquisition Strategy

### Target Customer Profiles

#### Profile 1: Research Institution
- **Size**: 500+ researchers
- **Need**: AI experiment coordination
- **Budget**: $50K-200K annually
- **Decision maker**: IT Director, Research VP
- **Sales cycle**: 6-12 months

#### Profile 2: Enterprise AI Team
- **Size**: 50+ AI/ML engineers
- **Need**: Internal agent orchestration
- **Budget**: $100K-500K annually
- **Decision maker**: VP Engineering, CTO
- **Sales cycle**: 6-9 months

#### Profile 3: AI Service Provider
- **Size**: Building AI products
- **Need**: Multi-agent workflow
- **Budget**: Usage-based
- **Decision maker**: Technical founder, CTO
- **Sales cycle**: 1-3 months

### Acquisition Channels

| Direct Sales | High | Enterprise outreach |
| Developer Community | High | Open source, content |
