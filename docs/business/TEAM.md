## Hiring Philosophy

1. **Technical Excellence**: Prioritize deep technical expertise over management experience in early stages
2. **Startup Mindset**: Seek candidates comfortable with ambiguity and rapid iteration
3. **Remote-First**: Build distributed team to access global talent
4. **Diversity**: Commit to building diverse team across all dimensions
5. **Ownership Culture**: Every team member should think like an owner
