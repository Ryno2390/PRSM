## 0. What this is

The Foundation jurisdiction scoping doc recommends Cayman Foundation
Company as primary, Swiss Verein as alternate. This doc is what you use
to pick counsel:

1. Pick 3 firms from §2 Cayman + 2 from §3 Swiss.
2. Copy the email template from §5.
3. Hit Send.

Every firm-level claim in §2–§3 cites a public 2025–2026 source. Pricing
and lead-time columns are blank because no firm publishes them — RFP
responses fill those in.

## 4. Decision criteria tuned to PRSM

When RFP responses come back, score each firm on these six axes (1-5):

| Axis | Why it matters for PRSM |
|------|------------------------|
| **Comparable-protocol formation track record** | Walkers → Arbitrum is the single public match. Firms without comparable Foundation-level work are learning on PRSM's dime. |
| **In-house professional-director capability** | US founder ECI-risk mitigation requires a professional Cayman director (see jurisdiction scoping §2.1). Firms with in-house fiduciary arms (WPS / MaplesFS / Ogier Global / Carey Olsen Corp Services / Campbells Corp) simplify the engagement; outsourced directors add coordination cost. |
| **VASP Act interpretation depth** | FTNS profile needs a clean opinion that utility-token issuance via a Foundation Company falls outside VASP licensing. Firms with published 2025-2026 VASP Act work (Maples, Carey Olsen) are ahead. |
| **US-founder ECI / CFC mitigation experience** | Unadvertised publicly but asked directly via RFP. Firms that have structured this pattern before quote tighter and faster. |
| **Turnaround from RFP to formation start** | Foundation formation gates everything downstream (audit engagement, Safe ownership transfer, governance activation). Firms with 2-4 week turn vs. 8-12 week queue matter. |
| **First-year compliance bundled vs. per-matter billing** | Must confirm whether quote includes first-year compliance (registered office, supervisor fee, annual filings) or is formation-only. |

**Recommended top-3 (Cayman path), ordered:**

1. **Walkers** — only firm with a publicly verifiable comparable
   Foundation + in-house professional-director capability. Best default.
2. **Carey Olsen** — VASP Act substance leader. Send as a parallel
   comparison quote + potential VASP opinion source even if Walkers does
   the formation.
3. **Maples Group** — market-standard workhorse. MaplesFS at scale. The
   authoritative VASP Amendment Act 2025 publication is an anchor.

Send RFP to all three in parallel. Compare on duration, cost,
communication cadence, professional-director included, remediation
terms, and partner seniority.

**Swiss (if pivoting):** MME + Bär & Karrer in parallel; decide after
pitch.

Hi {{FIRM_CONTACT_NAME}},

I'm Ryne Schultz, founder of PRSM (Protocol for Recursive Scientific

utility token and its on-chain treasury, and {{FIRM_NAME}} is on our

=== What we're asking ===

2. Fixed-fee quote (preferred) or hourly estimate for:
   - Cayman Foundation Company formation (if applicable to your offering)

will be the counterparty on that engagement.

Happy to jump on a 30-minute intro call to walk through the jurisdiction
scoping doc's §4 recommendation and §5 decision-memo template.

Best,
Ryne Schultz
schultzryne@gmail.com
{{GITHUB_PROFILE_URL}}

## 7. Items to verify during RFP (could not pin from public sources)

- **Exact comparable-protocol engagements** beyond Walkers → Arbitrum.
  Ask each firm directly for redacted matter descriptions.
- **Fixed-fee formation cost + first-year compliance bundling.** No firm
  publishes; 2024-2025 public rate cards suggest $15-40K formation +
  $15-35K/yr compliance, but require live quote.
- **Professional-director annual cost** — typically $10-25K/yr in-house;
  confirm per firm.
- **Conflicts** — Filecoin Foundation and Opentensor Foundation do not
  publicly name counsel. Must request conflict checks directly.
- **US-founder ECI/CFC mitigation track record** — never publicly
  advertised. Ask for anonymized matter descriptions during pitch.
- **MME engagements beyond Ethereum** — often-repeated "MME did Tezos /
  Cosmos / Cardano" claim is not on MME's own website. Treat as
  speculative until the firm confirms directly.

## 8. What this doc does NOT do

- **Does not commit PRSM to any firm.** Decision after RFP responses.
- **Does not substitute for counsel advice** once engaged.
- **Does not address scope for secondary entities** (e.g., a Delaware
  grants-arm paired with the Cayman Foundation, or an issuer SPV via
  VISTA Trust). Scope those separately post-primary-engagement.
