# PRSM Council Resolution PRSM-CR-2026-05-06-1

**Resolution ID:** PRSM-CR-2026-05-06-1
**Type:** Foundation Council Resolution — sole-founder ratification
**Issued:** 2026-05-06
**Subject:** Ratification of PRSM-POL-1 (Treasury Reserve & Risk-

---

## 1. Authority

The PRSM Foundation council, currently composed of a single member
(the founder, pending expansion to 2-of-3 per the governance charter),
adopts this resolution under the authority granted by:

- `docs/2026-04-21-prsm-gov-1-foundation-governance-charter.md` —
  Foundation governance charter, council role + voting rules.
- `docs/governance/2026-05-05-treasury-reserve-and-risk-transfer-policy.md`
  §13 — which permits ratification "by equivalent council resolution
  referencing this document by ID PRSM-POL-1 + commit hash."

At the present council size (1), council unanimity (1-of-1) is the
operative quorum. This is the same standard the council will apply
to itself at sizes 2-of-3 (or higher, on later expansion) under §10
amendment process.

## 2. Resolved

**RESOLVED**, that PRSM-POL-1 (Treasury Reserve & Risk-Transfer
Policy) is hereby **ratified** at the five parameter values
proposed in §12 of the policy as drafted on 2026-05-05:

| Parameter | Ratified value |
|---|---|
| Floor reserve (§3.1) | **$2,000,000 USD-equivalent** |
| Target reserve (§3.2) | **$10,000,000 USD-equivalent** |
| Single-user position trigger (§4.2 #1) | **1% of treasury** |
| Disbursement authorization tiers (§5.2) | **As drafted** (< $10K founder + 1 signer; $10K–$100K + 7-day notice; $100K–$500K + 14-day notice; > $500K + 30-day notice + member vote when chartered) |
| Annual allocation (§6) | **40% Operations / 30% Self-insurance / 20% Strategic / 10% External coverage premium** |

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that this ratification takes effect
**immediately** (2026-05-06) and binds the Foundation Safe at
`0x91b0e6F85A371D82De94eD13A3812d9f5A4E5791` to operate within the
disbursement-authorization framework defined in PRSM-POL-1 §5
beginning on the effective date.

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that PRSM-POL-1 §11 (Audit & compliance)
review cadence is hereby calendared:
- Quarterly operational adherence review by founder + council —
  next review: 2026-08-06
- Annual review by Foundation counsel — first review upon L8 Track A
  (Cayman) counsel engagement
- Material-amendment cross-jurisdictional review — per amendment

## 3. Council expansion commitment

The Foundation council commits to expanding to **2-of-3** members
within twelve (12) months of this resolution, by 2027-05-06. Upon
expansion, this resolution will be **re-confirmed** by the full
council under §13 ("2-of-3 council signature") to remove the
"pending council expansion" qualifier on the policy header.

In the interim, the founder will:
- Maintain a council-candidate shortlist (D7 deputy-founder + L6d
  insider-threat policy already establish jurisdictional and
  conflict constraints on candidates)
- Report quarterly on council-expansion progress alongside the
  L6e operational hygiene review

If 12 months elapses without expansion, the founder shall publish a
written extension memo with rationale, and the policy steward duty
shall remain with the founder pending Foundation Director of
Engineering hire (per PRSM-POL-1 §13).

## 7. Future amendments

This resolution may be amended only by a subsequent Foundation council
resolution that:

1. Identifies this resolution by ID `PRSM-CR-2026-05-06-1`.
2. States the change being made (e.g., "amends Floor reserve from
   $2M to $5M").
3. Cites the rationale and any §11 review findings supporting the
   change.
4. Is adopted under the council-quorum rules in effect at the time
   of amendment (currently 1-of-1; will be 2-of-3 post-expansion;
   per §10 of the policy, material amendments to §3 / §4 / §5
   thresholds also require 30-day public notice).
