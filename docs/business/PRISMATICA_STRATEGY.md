## What Is Prismatica?

Prismatica Inc. is the for-profit commercial arm of the PRSM ecosystem. It exists to solve a fundamental bootstrapping problem for any decentralized network: **who provides reliable infrastructure before third-party nodes join at scale?**

The answer is Prismatica. As the PRSM network's primary **mega-node operator**, Prismatica supplies the base-load compute, data, and storage capacity that the network depends on during early growth. In exchange, Prismatica earns **FTNS provenance royalties** — a contractual claim on the network's native token for every resource unit it contributes.

This is not a speculative position. It is a **structural royalty stake** in the PRSM network, earned through the actual provision of infrastructure.

## The Mega-Node Model

### What a Mega-Node Does

In the PRSM network, any participant that contributes compute, data, or storage earns FTNS provenance royalties proportional to their contribution. Mega-nodes are large-scale contributors that serve as the network's backbone — providing consistent, high-availability resources that smaller nodes cannot guarantee.

Prismatica is designed to be the **anchor mega-node** in the early network, responsible for:

- **Base-load compute**: GPU/CPU cycles for AI inference and model execution
- **Base-load data**: Curated, high-quality datasets made available to network participants
- **Base-load storage**: Distributed storage capacity for model weights, embeddings, and outputs

As third-party nodes join the network over time, Prismatica's share of total supply may decrease proportionally — but total FTNS demand grows with the network, meaning Prismatica's absolute royalty earnings can grow even as its relative share shifts.

### Why Mega-Node, Not Just a Node

A standard node earns royalties proportional to its contribution. A mega-node earns royalties at scale — and more importantly, it **ensures the network has a reliable floor of capacity** at all times. This is critical for:

1. **Enterprise adoption**: Enterprise customers require SLA-backed availability; Prismatica provides this floor
2. **New node onboarding**: The network needs to be functional before small nodes join; Prismatica ensures it is
3. **Network credibility**: Demonstrated, high-volume infrastructure operation signals technical seriousness to partners, researchers, and future node operators

## The FTNS Royalty Flywheel

The core of Prismatica's business model is a **royalty flywheel** driven by network growth:

```
More AI agents & users join PRSM
          │
          ▼
Demand for compute, data & storage increases
          │
          ▼
Prismatica earns more FTNS royalties
          │
          ▼
FTNS supply/demand tightens → token appreciates
          │
          ▼
Prismatica's treasury grows in value
          │
          ▼
Capital reinvested in more infrastructure capacity
          │
          ▼
More capacity → more royalties → flywheel continues
```

This model has three attractive properties:

1. **Passive accumulation**: Prismatica earns FTNS simply by running infrastructure — no active product sales required
2. **Non-dilutive growth**: Prismatica's treasury grows without selling equity or tokens at a loss
3. **Asymmetric upside**: Early infrastructure contributions earn royalties at lower FTNS valuations; appreciation is captured on the treasury

### Comparable Business Models

| Comparable | Similarity |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Early AWS** | Infrastructure provider for a platform ecosystem; captured massive value as the ecosystem grew around it |
| **ARM Holdings** | Royalties on a chip architecture; earns on every device using the platform without owning the end product |
| **Berkshire Hathaway** | Float model — capital compounds over time through structural position, not active trading |

Prismatica combines elements of all three: infrastructure provisioning (AWS), royalty accumulation (ARM), and treasury compounding (Berkshire).

## The Foundation / For-Profit Relationship

Prismatica Inc. is paired with the **PRSM Foundation**, a planned non-profit entity that governs the PRSM protocol and holds protocol IP.

```
PRSM Foundation (Non-Profit)
├── Governs the PRSM protocol
├── Holds protocol IP (trademarks, architecture)
├── Ensures network remains open and decentralized
├── Holds equity stake in Prismatica Inc.
└── Receives dividend distributions from Prismatica
         │
         └──► Funds Foundation operations, research, grants
```

This structure serves two purposes:

**1. Credibility and trust**: The PRSM protocol is governed by a neutral non-profit, not by a commercial entity with a profit motive. This is essential for attracting third-party node operators, research institutions, and enterprise customers who need assurance that the network cannot be captured or closed.

**2. Self-sustaining funding**: The Foundation holds equity in Prismatica and receives dividends. This means protocol governance is funded through commercial success rather than perpetual fundraising or token sales. As Prismatica grows, the Foundation grows with it — structurally aligned without being commercially entangled.

**Precedents**: Mozilla Foundation / Mozilla Corporation, Ethereum Foundation, Linux Foundation.

## Why Investors Should Care

### The Investment Proposition

**Investing in Prismatica Inc. is equivalent to buying a royalty position on the PRSM network.**

- Every compute job, data query, and storage operation on the PRSM network flows through FTNS
- Prismatica has a structural, early-mover claim on those royalties for the resources it contributes
- As the network scales, Prismatica's royalty position appreciates — not through dilution of other shareholders, but through network-wide growth

### Key Investor Advantages

| Advantage | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Early royalty position** | Prismatica earns at lower FTNS valuations and holds through appreciation |
| **No revenue ceiling from sales** | Revenue is bounded only by network size, not sales team capacity |
| **Network effects work for you** | Each new node operator increases demand for FTNS and benefits Prismatica's holdings |
| **Defensible position** | Mega-node infrastructure is capital-intensive to replicate; Prismatica's lead compounds |
| **Foundation alignment** | PRSM Foundation's protocol stewardship protects the open network that Prismatica's royalties depend on |

### Risk Considerations

- **Network adoption risk**: Prismatica's royalties are only valuable if the PRSM network achieves adoption. This is mitigated by PRSM's open-source distribution, SDK availability, and the economic inevitability of AI coordination infrastructure.
- **FTNS valuation risk**: Royalty earnings are denominated in FTNS. Token value is subject to market dynamics. Mitigated by accumulation strategy (hold, don't sell) and treasury management.
- **Entity formation risk**: Prismatica is not yet formed. This is a near-term action item with a clear path (Stripe Atlas, Delaware C-Corp).

## Summary

Prismatica Inc. is built on a simple, durable thesis: **the entity that provides reliable infrastructure for a growing network earns a compounding royalty position in that network.**

The PRSM network needs infrastructure to grow. Prismatica provides it. The network rewards Prismatica in FTNS. FTNS appreciates with the network. Prismatica's treasury grows. The PRSM Foundation — Prismatica's shareholder — receives dividends that fund open protocol governance in perpetuity.

This is not a bet on a product launch or a sales cycle. It is a **structural position in AI coordination infrastructure**, taken early, held long.
