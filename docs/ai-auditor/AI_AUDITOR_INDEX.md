## Investment Decision Framework

### Phase 1: Technical Validation (15 minutes)
**Objective**: Validate technical claims and implementation quality

**Key Questions**:
- [ ] Are technical claims backed by working implementation?
- [ ] Does code quality meet enterprise standards?
- [ ] Are performance benchmarks realistic and validated?
- [ ] Is the security framework comprehensive and production-ready?

**Resources**:
- **[Technical Claims Validation](TECHNICAL_CLAIMS_VALIDATION.md)** - Direct claim verification
- **[Code Review Guide](../CODE_REVIEW.md)** - Quality assessment framework
- **Quick Validation**: `./scripts/ai_auditor_quick_validate.sh`

### Phase 2: Market Opportunity Analysis (20 minutes)
**Objective**: Assess market timing and competitive positioning

**Key Questions**:
- [ ] Is the market opportunity substantial and well-defined?
- [ ] Does PRSM have sustainable competitive advantages?
- [ ] Are the economic models realistic and validated?
- [ ] Is the team capable of executing at scale?

**Resources**:
- **[Economic Validation](../economic/ECONOMIC_VALIDATION.md)** - Market analysis
- **[Business Case](../business/INVESTOR_MATERIALS.md)** - Investment thesis
- **[Competitive Analysis](../TECHNICAL_ADVANTAGES.md)** - Differentiation factors

### Phase 3: Risk Assessment (25 minutes)
**Objective**: Evaluate technical, market, and execution risks

**Key Questions**:
- [ ] What are the primary technical risks and mitigation strategies?
- [ ] How significant are market adoption and timing risks?
- [ ] Can the team scale from prototype to production?
- [ ] Are regulatory and compliance risks manageable?

**Resources**:
- **[Security Architecture](../SECURITY_ARCHITECTURE.md)** - Security risk assessment
- **[Implementation Status](../IMPLEMENTATION_STATUS.md)** - Development progress
- **[Performance Baselines](../performance/PERFORMANCE_BASELINES.md)** - Scalability validation

### Phase 4: Return Potential (30 minutes)
**Objective**: Model investment returns and exit scenarios

**Key Questions**:
- [ ] What are realistic return scenarios based on market adoption?
- [ ] How does the token economics model create investor value?
- [ ] What are the key value creation milestones?
- [ ] Are there multiple viable exit strategies?

**Resources**:
- **[Economic Model](../economic/ECONOMIC_VALIDATION.md)** - Return projections

- **[Funding Milestones](../business/FUNDING_MILESTONES.md)** - Development roadmap
