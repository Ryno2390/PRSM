## 2. Background

### 2.1 Current state (verified 2026-05-13)

Phase 5 fiat surface is commission-ready (sprints 276-286 closed). The `coinbase_offramp_initiate` composer returns `status: "PENDING_COMMISSION"` because the live USDC↔FTNS rate is unavailable — no on-chain Aerodrome pool yet exists for the pair. Operators currently fall back to `PRSM_FTNS_USD_RATE` env-var-set static rate per `prsm/node/api.py:840` — a documented bootstrap trust seam (audit-prep §7.23) gated on this ceremony.

### 2.2 PRSM-CR-2026-05-08 §Resolution 8 deferral

`PRSM-CR-2026-05-08.md` §Resolution 8 explicitly deferred the funding-source decision (Foundation treasury vs Prismatica balance sheet). Today's resolution closes that deferral.

### 2.3 Architectural reasoning

Pool seeding is commercial activity:
- Sets a public market price (commercial signal)
- Creates a fee-bearing LP position (commercial product)
- Bootstraps a marketplace where Prismatica's commercial integrations (Coinbase CDP onramp/offramp, Aerodrome swap rails) operate

The two-entity structure (Foundation Cayman nonprofit / Prismatica DE C-corp) exists specifically to keep commercial activity on Prismatica's books. Funding the seed from Prismatica:

- Keeps Foundation out of Howey-factor "expectation of profits from the efforts of others" exposure — Foundation remains a passive LP-token holder receiving investment-style fee income.
- Aligns with Tokenomics §3.5 invariant ("Foundation seeds the protocol pool as a one-time bootstrap event; Foundation does NOT run continuous market-making"). The seed is commercial; the post-seed pool is a public good open to any participant.
- Matches the architectural precedent in `docs/2026-04-27-cumulative-audit-prep.md` §7 (Helium / io.net DePIN pool-launch pattern).
- Respects PRSM-POL-1 capital husbandry — Foundation reserves are not drained for what is fundamentally a Prismatica-beneficiary expense.

The FTNS side of the seed comes from Foundation Safe's existing 100M FTNS treasury (per `project_mainnet_bringup_path_a_complete_2026_05_06.md`). 2M FTNS = 2% of treasury. The 2% allocation is consistent with one-time bootstrap accounting; no PRSM-POL-1 §5 disbursement-tier escalation required (tier-1 ≤ 1M FTNS / tier-2 ≤ 10M FTNS / tier-3 > 10M; 2M sits in tier-2).

### 2.4 Sepolia rehearsal status

Per ceremony plan §3.6 + §10, this ceremony is gated on a Sepolia rehearsal completing within 7 days preceding mainnet execution. The rehearsal runbook is at `docs/governance/2026-06-15-aerodrome-pool-seed-sepolia-rehearsal.md`. Rehearsal artifacts (bundle file, Basescan tx hashes, screenshots of hardware-wallet device screens) will be archived as `docs/governance/2026-06-XX-aerodrome-sepolia-rehearsal-lessons.md` upon completion.

**This resolution becomes operative only after the rehearsal lessons doc has been committed to the repo.** The founder confirms rehearsal completion as part of the ratification signature (§5 below).

### 2.5 Prismatica USDC wire prerequisite

This resolution authorizes the on-chain ceremony but does NOT authorize the Prismatica → Foundation Safe USDC wire. The wire is a prior commercial transaction between Prismatica (DE C-corp) and Foundation (Cayman nonprofit), requiring its own corporate authorization on the Prismatica side (Prismatica board resolution or operating-agreement-authorized founder signature, depending on Prismatica's governance). The wire MUST be completed and verified on Basescan **before** the ceremony executes; verification is part of the pre-flight checklist in ceremony plan §3.4.

## 3. Ratification scope

The council ratifies the following six binding decisions:

### 3.1 Funding source

**Prismatica (DE C-corp) funds the USDC side.** Prismatica wires $500,000 USDC (or USDC-equivalent) to Foundation Safe `0x91b0000000000000000000000000000000005791` via a Base-mainnet USDC transfer. Verification of receipt on Basescan is a pre-flight gate per ceremony plan §3.4.

The USDC, once received by Foundation Safe, is treated as a Foundation asset for the duration of the seed transaction. Resulting LP tokens are owned by Foundation Safe. No Prismatica claim against the LP tokens or any future fee income is recognized by this resolution.

### 3.2 Seed amounts (locked)

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| USDC seed amount | **$500,000** (= 500_000_000_000 wei; USDC uses 6 decimals) |
| FTNS seed amount | **2,000,000 FTNS** (= 2_000_000 × 10^18 wei = 2_000_000_000_000_000_000_000_000 wei) |
| Implied bootstrap price | **$0.25 / FTNS** |
| Pool type | Aerodrome VOLATILE (`stable=false`) — xy=k constant-product |
| LP-token recipient | Foundation Safe `0x91b0000000000000000000000000000000005791` |

These amounts are within the Tokenomics §3.7 envelope ($250K-$1M USDC; matched FTNS). Deviation from these locked amounts at ceremony time requires a superseding resolution.

### 3.3 Implied price rationale

$0.25/FTNS is a defensible bootstrap-announcement price, not a market-attested price. The founder council acknowledges:

- The seed price **will be challenged** by arbitrageurs once the pool is open. The price after the first arbitrage cycle is the market price, not the seed price.
- The $0.25 figure derives from comparable DePIN-token reference range ($0.10-$1.00) and from PRSM's pre-bootstrap implied valuation as expressed in pre-raise materials. It is NOT a price floor, a buyback commitment, or a guarantee.
- Communications referencing the seed price MUST use language explicitly framing it as a bootstrap parameter (e.g., "Foundation seeded at $0.25/FTNS; ongoing market price determined by independent participants"), NOT as a current valuation.

### 3.4 Authorization for deploy gas spend

Foundation authorizes operational expenditure of up to **0.05 ETH on Base mainnet** for the ceremony's gas costs (3 txs × ~250K gas per tx ceiling). Source of gas funds: Foundation Safe's ETH balance.

### 3.5 Pre-commitment to ceremony-plan §10 cancellation criteria

The cancellation tree in ceremony plan §10 is pre-ratified. The founder may abort the ceremony for any of the documented reasons without additional council approval. Specifically:

- Pool already exists (frontrunner) → abort, escalate to follow-up CR
- Foundation Safe insufficient balance (USDC or FTNS) → abort
- Hardware-wallet signature failure after 3 retries → abort
- Sepolia rehearsal expired (>7 days) → abort, reschedule

## 4. Explicit non-scope

The following are NOT authorized by this resolution:

1. **Continuous market-making.** Foundation provides initial liquidity once; does NOT MM continuously (Tokenomics §3.5 invariant; carry-over from PRSM-CR-2026-05-08 §Resolution 3).
2. **LP-token transfers.** Foundation Safe holds the LP tokens; no authorization to transfer them out exists. Any future transfer requires a new CR.
3. **Liquidity withdrawal.** Removing liquidity from the pool (`Router.removeLiquidity`) is not authorized; requires its own CR.
4. **veAERO gauge whitelisting.** Separate governance ceremony; gated on Foundation acquiring a veAERO position. This CR does not authorize that acquisition.
5. **Coinbase CDP commissioning.** Sibling external-party engagement; this ceremony is necessary but not sufficient.
6. **Composite operations.** No interactions with Aerodrome's gauge / voter contracts beyond `Router.addLiquidity`.
7. **Pre-mortem of failed ceremony attempts.** Any ceremony abort triggers a separate written post-mortem; this resolution does not pre-authorize a re-attempt.
8. **FTNS market sales by Foundation.** Foundation does not sell FTNS for USDC; the FTNS side comes from existing treasury, the USDC side from Prismatica wire.
9. **Authorization of Prismatica's corporate wire.** Prismatica's USDC transfer to Foundation Safe is a Prismatica-side action requiring Prismatica's own governance authorization; this resolution does not substitute for that.
10. **Future LP token utility.** The LP tokens become Foundation Safe property at ceremony completion. Their disposition (hold, transfer to gauge, stake to receive AERO emissions, etc.) is a future-CR decision, not pre-authorized here.
